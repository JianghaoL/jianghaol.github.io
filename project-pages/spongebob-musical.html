Overview

This musical was performed four times in April 2024.

I was the head of sound for this musical, in charge of sound tech, sound effects, and mic and other sound-related devices management.

The profit of the show was donated to help minority people.

Sound Implementation

With the help of our instructor Steffan Incze, we set up the whole theatre's sound system by placing a mixer backstage. All sounds go into the mixer and get premixed, where we EQ, compress and limit them. This mixer has two output routes, one goes into the on-stage monitor system, while the other one goes to the theater's PA system and the signal is thus amplified and played through the theater speakers.

For sound effects specifically, I used a MIDI keyboard and Mainstage program to trigger them. Each sound effect was mapped into a particular key on the keyboards, either one-shot or looping. I made my own cue sheet using the script, and played the sounds as the cue sheet indicated.
